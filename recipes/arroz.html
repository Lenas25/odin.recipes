Arroz con Pollo Recipe

Arroz con pollo is a dish of chicken and rice that originated in Spain. It is popular in many Latin American countries, and is also found in other parts of the world, including the United States.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Rice
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Chili

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken
  2. Marinate the chicken
  3. Add the vegetables and rice
  4. Marinate the rice
  5. Let it sit
