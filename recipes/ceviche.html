Ceviche Recipe

Ceviche is a seafood dish that originated in Peru. It is made with raw fish or seafood that is marinated in citrus juices, such as lemon or lime. The dish is popular in many Latin American countries, and is also found in other parts of the world, including the United States.

Ingredients

  • Raw fish
  • Lime
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Chili

Instructions

  1. Cut the fish
  2. Marinate the fish
  3. Add the vegetables
  4. Let it sit
  5. Enjoy
